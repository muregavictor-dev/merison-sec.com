Comprehensive Security & Energy Solutions

At MERISON Systems, we go beyond basic installations. We provide advanced, customized solutions designed to ensure safety, efficiency, and convenience for both residential and commercial clients.

Our services integrate the latest innovations to give you reliable, long-term security and energy solutions.

CCTV Installation & Maintenance

In today's world, security is a necessity. Our high-resolution CCTV systems offer round-the-clock monitoring with features such as night vision, motion detection, and remote access via mobile apps. We assess your premises, design a surveillance layout, install top-tier cameras, and provide ongoing maintenance to keep your security flawless.

Security System Solutions

Security extends beyond cameras. We provide comprehensive solutions including biometric access control for restricted areas, smart alarm systems integrated with emergency response units, motion sensors, and intercom systems. Our goal is to create an impenetrable security framework that protects what matters most.

Solar Panel Installation & Energy Solutions

Embrace renewable energy with our state-of-the-art solar power solutions. We conduct energy audits to design a system that meets your consumption needs while maximizing efficiency. Our services include on-grid and off-grid solar panel installations, battery storage systems, and hybrid solar solutions, ensuring reliable power even in remote locations.

Modern Gate Automation & Access Control

Step into the future with smart gate automation. We install automated sliding, swinging, and telescopic gates equipped with remote control access, fingerprint and RFID entry, and AI-powered license plate recognition. Whether for homes, businesses, or gated communities, our solutions provide security and ease of access at the highest level.
